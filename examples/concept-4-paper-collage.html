About (the part where I talk about myself)

Hello, it's me

I'm Jules, a designer based in a small studio that's honestly mostly plants and coffee cups at this point. I make things for print and screen -- identities, books, websites, exhibitions, the occasional zine nobody asked for.

I believe design should feel like something. Not polished to death, but alive, a little rough around the edges. Like a letter from a friend instead of an email from a brand.

When I'm not designing, I'm probably at a flea market buying old postcards or trying to learn letterpress (again).
